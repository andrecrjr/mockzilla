# Browser Extension Sync

The Mockzilla Chrome Extension is your local assistant. It lets you capture real API requests directly from your browser and turn them into permanent mocks in your Mockzilla Dashboard.

## One-Click Synchronization

Once you've configured your mocks in the browser extension, you can sync them with your server instantly.

### Why Sync?
- **Backup:** Save your local browser mocks to the server.
- **Teamwork:** Share your mocks with teammates.
- **Deployment:** Use your mocks in a CI/CD environment.

---

## 🛡️ Smart Merge (Zero Data Loss)

Mockzilla's "Smart Merge" strategy means you never have to worry about losing your hard work.

- **Local to Server:** We push your browser rules to the server and organize them into "Extension" folders.
- **Server to Local:** We pull your server mocks back into your browser. If you've added local-only features (like custom delays or status overrides), Mockzilla **preserves them** even after a sync.

---

## How to Sync

1. Open the Mockzilla Chrome Extension in your browser.
2. Click the **Sync** icon in the header.
3. Choose **Push** to save your local mocks to the dashboard.
4. Choose **Import** to bring dashboard mocks into your browser.

> **Pro Tip:** Intercept real-world APIs, save them to Mockzilla, and then edit the response to test edge cases without ever needing the real backend!
